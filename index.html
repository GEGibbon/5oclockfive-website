About

A sharp, crowd-focused live band

5 O'Clock Five brings a polished mix of party classics, modern favorites, and flexible setlists tailored to the room. The band is equally comfortable turning a wedding reception into a packed dance floor, supporting a private celebration, or bringing a public event to life.

With keys, guitar, sax, bass, drums, and vocals, the group can shape the night around cocktails, dinner, dancing, headline sets, and custom event pacing.
